BeautyLab wins more awards
==========================

 Published: 11-Nov-2025 **Featured companies:**[**BEAUTYLAB**](https://cosmeticsbusiness.com/beautylab-155156)

[Skin Care](https://cosmeticsbusiness.com/tag/skin-care)

Renowned for its transformative, performance-led formulations, BeautyLab captures two Platinum Magazine Beauty Awards

Over the years, BeautyLab has won numerous skincare awards, and has just received two more at the 2025 Platinum Magazine Beauty Awards. The judging panel was formed of industry experts (dermatologists, makeup artists and aestheticians) as well as trusted Platinum Beauty Magazine readers. The products were rigorously tested to identify those that delivered the most exceptional results and best value for money.

### Black Diamond Pearl Dermabrasion

![Image 2: BeautyLab wins more awards](https://cosmeticsbusiness.com/article-image-alias/beautylab-wins-more-awards-2.jpg)

_Winner: Best Exfoliator_

A luxurious exfoliating treatment that boosts cell renewal, promotes skin smoothness, enhances hydration and targets pigmentation – for a

 vibrant and healthier looking complexion.

Key actives include:

*   Diamonds
*   Mother of Pearl
*   Glycolic Acid
*   Lactic Acid

_Ideal for skin that is dry, aged/prematurely aged, lacking resilience_

RRP 50ml: £78 / €94 / $105

### BeautyLab Hydrating Mask

![Image 3: BeautyLab wins more awards](https://cosmeticsbusiness.com/article-image-alias/beautylab-wins-more-awards-3.jpg)

_Winner: Best Face Mask_

An intensive treatment mask, designed to combat dryness, flaky skin and the signs of ageing. It deeply hydrates, promotes elasticity and helps to smooth fine lines and wrinkles – for a more supple and resilient complexion.

Key actives include:

*   Collagen Tripeptide
*   Acai Berry
*   Seaweed Extract
*   Radish Root Peptide
*   Rosemary
*   Bitter Orange
*   Aloe Vera

_Ideal for all skin, including skin lacking that is dull/uneven in tone._

RRP 50ml: £34 / €44 / $49

Also available in a 15ml try-me size.

_“These awards validate our team’s commitment to creating advanced, high-performance skin products that deliver transformative results for customers everywhere. The core mission has always been to set an elevated standard in skincare, and we’re thrilled to be recognised by industry experts and consumers alike”_ said Roger Aoun, BeautyLab Founder.

Further information:

UK: info@gerrardinternational.com | gerrardinternational.com

International: info@beautylabinternational.com | beautylabinternational.com
